Steven Cueto is a Front-end Software Developer. Before becoming a Software Developer Steven was a General Physician for Urgent Care facilities in the Dominican Republic, working across multiple resorts, mainly Punta Cana sales activities, working with from all over the world.

Steven Cueto is a Front-end Developer. Born in La Romana, Dominican Republic in 1993. He Grew up as a happy introverted kid who liked schools, and films a bit too much.

He graduated cum laude from Universidad Central Del Este (UCE) in San Pedro de Macoris, Dominican Republic, with an M.D. Doctor of Medicine. He's currently doing the Software Engineering Immersive Bootcamp in General Assembly as of March 2022.

When Steven was in his intern year for Medicine, he worked as an Assistant for a prestigious Neurosurgeon in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. In 2019, while working as a General Physician in Urgent Care facilities in the Dominican Republic, he won the Doctor Of the Year Award. He worked as a Covid Sample Collector for cast and crew for NBC TV productions.

Undergoing health complications in 2020 and 2021, Steven reflected on his lifestyle and decided to change careers to have better a work-life balance. So he went into Software Development, to combine his drive to help people while also building new things and being challenged. Steven moved from Punta Cana to New York City in March 2021, after getting married in January 2021.

Steven speaks Spanish and English. In his free time, he enjoys: cooking, going to church, listening to music, watching films and anime, and learning new things.
